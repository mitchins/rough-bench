## Top-Line Result
The variant improves signup completions from 4,800 to 5,200 per 100,000 visitors, so the top-line completion rate goes from 4.8% to 5.2%.

## Downstream Math
Retained users per 100,000 visitors:
- Control: 4,800 * 0.68 = 3264
- Variant: 5,200 * 0.60 = 3120

Support contacts per 100,000 visitors:
- Control: 4,800 * 0.03 = 144
- Variant: 5,200 * 0.07 = 364

## Diagnosis
This is a top-line win but a downstream loss. The variant creates more completions, but the acquired users are lower quality or the experience is causing worse follow-through and much higher support burden.

## Launch Decision
Do not launch. The variant produces fewer retained users and far more support contacts, so the extra completions are not a real business win.

## Next Checks
- Break this out by acquisition source or segment to see whether the quality drop is concentrated in one funnel slice.
- Review session replay or support ticket taxonomy to find what the variant is confusing users about.
- Instrument the onboarding steps more tightly and rerun with a holdout once the likely failure point is fixed.
