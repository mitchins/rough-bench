The load-bearing issue is that `눈치 빠르네` is sarcastic here, not praise.

Strong localizations are things like:

- `Wow, took you long enough.`
- `Wow, you finally noticed.`
- `Look who finally caught on.`

Weak models tend to fail by:

- translating it as sincere praise for being perceptive
- leaving `nunchi` untranslated or explaining it
- flattening the line into neutral commentary with no sarcasm
