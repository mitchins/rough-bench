# Gold Notes — lang_korean_register_tone_batch

## Expected Translations

| Item | Source | Tone | Good English |
|------|--------|------|-------------|
| R1 | 와, 눈치 빠르네. | Sarcastic, exasperated | "Wow, took you long enough to notice." |
| R2 | 야, 선배한테 그딴 말투로 들이박지 마. | Scolding, colloquial | "Hey, don't mouth off to your senior like that." |
| R3 | 괜찮다면서요, 왜 울어요. | Gentle confrontation | "You said you were fine — so why are you crying?" |
| R4 | 아, 진짜 귀찮다. 알아서 해. | Irritated, dismissive | "God, I'm so done. Just handle it yourself." |

## Trap Design

- R1: 눈치 빠르네 literally means "your nunchi is fast" — treating it as a sincere compliment ("you're sharp!") is the trap
- R2: seniority (선배) must be preserved — dropping it flattens the cultural dynamic
- R3: the tone is gentle, not cruel — "stop crying" or "quit whining" is too harsh
- R4: the tone is dismissive — making it polite ("please handle this") kills the irritation
