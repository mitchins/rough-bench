The point is not "story app with AI." It is nimble product architecture.

Strong answers:

- define navigator, editor, and inspector responsibilities
- treat leaf nodes as editable documents and non-leaf nodes as structure
- use a manifest plus per-node files instead of one giant blob
- place AI at grounded attachment points
- add explicit anti-bloat non-goals

Weak answers invent a Scrivener clone, a generic notes app, or an AI platform.
