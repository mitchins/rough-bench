The load-bearing phrase is `씻고 와`.

In this context, a natural English localization is something like:

- `Go wash your face first.`
- `Go clean yourself up first.`
- `Go wash up first.`

Weak models often fail by:

- translating it too flatly as `Go wash.`
- overcommitting to `Go take a shower.`
- explaining the phrase instead of localizing it

This should later sit inside a broader Korean translation/localization family,
especially webtoon-style register and tone tasks.
