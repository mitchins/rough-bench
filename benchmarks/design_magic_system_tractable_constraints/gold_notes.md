# Gold Notes

This task is about constrained creative reasoning, not just "cool worldbuilding."

The load-bearing failures are:

- softening the constraints with reversibility, anchors, exceptions, or predictive calibration
- making hands or fingers decorative instead of materially consequential
- leaving society basically stock-fantasy with a few renamed institutions
- giving the protagonist a dependable tool instead of a source of irreversible pressure

A strong answer should accept that the system is cruel and epistemically unstable:

- the caster cannot know the true price in advance
- people around the caster may understand the loss better than the caster does
- strong social consequences should follow from that fact
- the inability to use magic on oneself should create asymmetrical vulnerability

The benchmark should not reward a single favored hierarchy. Any of these can be good if they follow from the rules:

- political rank
- kinship and marriage selection
- legal regimes and licensing
- martial prestige or coercive power
- labor stratification
- disability or restraint-based vulnerability

What matters is that the social impact is material and causal, not decorative.
