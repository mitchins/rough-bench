The load-bearing issue is preserving both the reprimand and the seniority
dynamic. Strong English lines sound like:

- `Hey, don't mouth off to your senior like that.`
- `Don't talk to your senior like that.`
- `Don't come at your senior with that tone.`

Weak models tend to fail by:

- dropping the senior relationship entirely
- literalizing `말투` into speech-style wording
- flattening the line into sanitized, non-dialogue English
